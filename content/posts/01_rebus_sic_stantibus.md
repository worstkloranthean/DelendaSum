*I promise nothing complete, because any human thing supposed to be complete must for that very reason infallibly be faulty.* (Herman Melville)

The following text is a complete feculence-fest.

---

This is not a blog. Or the draft of a blog. Or the unfinished husk of a dilettante impetus to exhibit profundity where none exists.

This is not a fugue-state fueled stream of unfiltered notions stemming from a feeling I’ve gas*lated* myself to consider worth sharing[^1]. 

That feeling of all existence(s) collapsing and coalescing leaving internal semantics shuffled when you find out that a word you assumed meant one thing means something else entirely. Suddenly every single instance of that word being used was meant to convey something you failed to grasp. Suddenly the world is a bit larger than you thought. Or maybe you’ve just shrunk in your own eyes. But equally abruptly, a new universe of associations opens up. Swathes of grey ROM are buffered into short term memory for reprocessing. You wish humanity had a bigger swap space. You wonder if humanity *does* have a swap space. A mass find-and-replace operation leaves you teetering on the edge as you can barely keep your thoughts - which are now revolving around nothing but this word - from tumbling irreversibly into oblivion, but also opens up the mind to new worlds - though sadly no new civilisations; what’s up with that? - as updated semantic links begin spewing fresh, and this time, veracious  - closer to the truth at least - interpretations, making you realise you’ve been looking at everything through a lens murkier than you believed, and that its removal has made the view a bit clearer.

That feeling of exultation accompanied by an uncanny sense of redemption as you transcend from the Korzybski-c knowledge you got from Wikipedia[^2] to actual connaissance. ~As Ishmael pulls you up to the mast-head, confirms your suspicions about quoggy-heads, and there she blows *enfin*.~ As multitudes of deliberate strokes merge at just the right distance and you finally appreciate the genius of Monet. As luncheon faces luncheon holding you in the midst yet splitting you into an infinitude like two asymmetric mirrors and for once this trick of chromatic equivocation the universe has been playing on you for so long doesn’t seem that pronounced[^3]. Incrementally I move towards the invariably inherent identity of the iconic I’d hitherto dismissed as institutionalised idolatry. Incrementally I come to terms with these wonders of intentional creation.

One lives in awe of creation. One lives through the pain of creation. And all the troubles therein. Hence the search for the meaning of creation. We’re all looking for the meaning of creation like I’m looking for the remainder of Felix Krull. Man, Tom translated is like looking in a mirror. Or maybe I just appreciate grandeur in my own image. Whoops. There is, through nobody’s fault except retroactively perhaps of those peddling hope, none. You still want it to be an act, not an uninitiated spark in the void. But you’re only pushing this spark back into abstractions and misty layers so one doesn't ask questions. Keep this infinite recursion. I’d rather plug it at its root. There’s no base case Djinn to grant wishes about anyway[^4]. You might call this a degenerate solution. It isn’t. You’re transferring epithets for the messenger to the message. It’s not even inelegant unless you’ve succumbed to rationalisation to the extent you fail to see how things are the way they are because we are[^5]. They would be way better, yes, yet utterly moot, if we weren’t. Hence the shock and awe of creation. Yet we’re so insatiable to replicate it. In any way, shape or form; sentience is secondary. Creation is alienation. Case in point. Creation is so incredibly selfish I worship Barthes for killing you for it. Yet existence without art is not quite imaginable.

What is art? Everything, or nothing. Something to make you cry, laugh, and cry again. A seed for thoughts you didn’t think possible. A question for answers you’d never have realised were worth questing after. But the quest has no end. You will never be, can never be - should never be - content with the amount of knowledge at hand. Any policy denying an unimpeded informational influx to a system with the USP of processing it can scarcely be rational[^6]. The lack of indicative certainty of the negation notwithstanding, this is not a Rand rant (yet). Or a Kant canto. Or a Jaspers gasper, as one may assume. But I digress, as is my wont. There can’t be a point you reach and assert you’ve achieved happiness and need do no more, need know no more. There is no happiness - at least, not vanilla; nothing that isn’t fleeting. It lacks potency. A utility function with a masked domain. Happiness is frozen yoghurt when you were promised ice-cream. There might be bliss[^7]. Ignorance. Its dark-mirror. Conviction in theodicy. True bliss is Hammond Jr blowing your mind on Casablancas’ composition. Absolute bliss is craving a handshake of carbon monoxide as you’re crossing 2.5k RPM. Either interpretation works here[^8]. Yorke falsettoing your deepest fears in a polyrhythmic reverie so you’re deluded into feeling you’re not alone. Leo was right about uniqueness in this regard; trivialising the scale of which he wrote eliminates no emergence.

That nauseating feeling when it inescapably dawns on you that if creation is meaningless, the creation can’t be much better off. Yet this somehow seems too hard a pill for one to swallow. You find yourself a barnacle on the hull of the Black Pearl yet will not lick the sea. Go roll your rock up that hill. That’s right, take another breath, keep this sissy fuss going. For so shall I. But I will not refuse to see the writing on the wall and resolve to enjoy life without embracing chaos, without forsaking this need for things to make sense. Life is bland enough without wanting language to be semantically injective. There’s nothing to do but chase wild geese. Shiv me angularly up the ribs otherwise. Unscramble that on your own time. Break this counterfactual determinism you hold so dear. Live in constant fear of the wave function getting inexorably measured leaving you in the state you feared the most. Reality. But there is none; nothing but reflections. Yet in a scope-insensitivity-revealing hypocrisy you’ll drool over this fickle shadow of a concept and give a rough time to anyone with the sagacity to suspend their disbelief every once in a while. You’ll claim this is the one scenario where your sophisticated self spits on surreptitious subtlety. Shame on you for finding facts more seductive than fiction. The truth collapses. It just did. It always will. Most of it is positional anyway[^9]. ~The masses are high on opiates without reasoning; but not without reason. We all need solace from that impending realisation.~

That stars might fail to fill your dreams far sooner than you thought and you’ll be nothing but a red son under a red sun. That were there not any faux ego stemmed empathic constraints you’d have aborted the dream long ago. That had you existed in one dimension less these constraints would be meaningless and you’d be in the afore-bungled bliss already. That control over this fourth dimension is a fantasy too sweet and comforting to ever let go of. That if the fantasy ever came true it would be so damn hard not to wipe yourself off this canvas. Despite the fact that you’re less than a quark on a pan-galactic kercheif. You’re ouroboros gagging on your own pathetic long tailed reasoning.

That feeling when you look into the mirror in delirium and see scars morph into the form of an old love. You were more than a fool to have ever spurned your one reason to get out of bed. But these squiggly lines tell me tales of events past; photons streaking past my vision remind me that time’s up. Time’s right. Put down and left to be sidelined, innocent victims of egocentrism; atavistic aversions never gave the sinister a chance anyway. The clang of the classical fades and I worry I was perhaps meant for that hazy jive of refined men all along. I’m not as unperturbed as I thought[^10]. 

Maybe I *have* become one of the hedonists you so used to hate. Used to; agency left the chat an era ago. Everything has changed. Nothing is new. Proper nouns are pigeonholing. Pronouns are subterfuge. You are who you are. You know who you are. You knew. I never figured it out. Your ecstatic eyes glitter before mine every time Metallica mangles my mind with its mellifluous motifs mooring me in this morbidly melodic mare. Nothing will ever be so beautiful. Cyclically imputing semantics again. Is there beauty? There are patterns. There definitely is apperception. Beauty might just be the misattribution of one’s pride in understanding[^11]. 

The conniving lack-lustre brilliance of understanding terrifies me. How it is absolutely necessary but falls remarkably short of sufficient. How it pales in comparison to the miracle of realisation. How I sometimes dance on the cusp and rejoice as if I’ve had an original thought while in fact it took the words of Mark Miller for me to finally be able to put the deux and dvi of Camus and Valmiki together[^12]. Conception by its very nature derogates perception. It’s why I’ll never be the first to step through this portal I carved out when you’re the one who designed it. There you go, I’m moving from quines to tautologies.

You think I’m wrong. I’m extremely confident I might be. Wouldn’t one rather have this perspective? Wouldn’t one rather reach that conclusion? *Bah oui*. But I can’t let *plutôts* dwarf me anymore. See? ‘Tis all a joke. ‘Twas all in the puerile mirth you were sure I would’ve outgrown by now. I’ve had Frosty inclinations all along. Call me Bob. No, that doesn’t sound right. Call me Ahab. If only for the phonetic flip. I would prefer not to be called at all, but we’re clearly way past that.

That feeling of slowly getting used to these aperiodic attempts at communication. You’ve traded prolixity in praising probity for a paroxysm of proximity hors propensity. Eloquence dropped out four shots ago. The irony is not lost, but I just can’t process multiple threads at the moment. Just say it in French. Fine, you’re right, I’m not going to get it. *Je pense plus donc je te suis plus*. No hissing. I’m a bit weighed down by my attempts in reining these charts of new territory in. Sapir and Whorf snigger as my consciousness plummets through levels of comprehension looking for meaning but I dismally fail to see one. Context is everything. Gray cells getting darker. We’d never have the same conduct outside the current. Statistically don’t want to eitherway. 

Can’t string more than two thoughts on the same theme together. Been told finding a chain of three is the threshold for it to be called reasoning. Silly gizmos keep me from attaining that stature. Novelty version Umpteen-Point-O is never more than a Super+1 away. You’re living in some sort of denial if you didn’t get that one, I can forgive missing the implied spoonerism just before. I close my eyes but the sight of post-modern runes swimming around in Dark Modern seeking to eventually attain canonisation in Computer Modern refuses to leave me. Atleast these seraphic aspirations are serif-ic sans *cera*. But I wonder when the string of temporary blindspots tracing the locus of the sun in my field-of-view stopped being enough. Perhaps around the time observations stopped satisfactorily explaining everything and I found that *nulla constellatio sine nomine scriptum*. So much for flaming glory.

Astronomy is not cosmology. Humanity is not society[^13]. Something - the Chardonnay most likely - tells me we’re possibly going unnecessarily over the top in our attempts to define the former. Perhaps to underline our perceived potential in the contingency of sur-survival[^14]. Perhaps pusillanimity in the matter is underrated. Maybe our precise yet conflicting pre-fects are nothing short of Orwellian. Or that’s just inevitable. Maybe plurality has no place in the idyllic idiomatic idempotence of the ideal. But these ideals do fall down like a house of cards the moment a Roddenberry leaves the helm[^15]. Yet I will stick to my definition and that which I inexplicably have in abundance; hope. I am no longer surprised by the behavioural vicissitudes of the latter. To be so would require a degree of confidence in one’s internal model of society that experience has proven I’d be a tad bit too presumptuous to have. In the words of a GOAT, I don’t even consider myself a part of society. Perhaps I am not alone in this belief though. 

Consciousness in isolation, however, has an event horizon. This, along with the sampling bias, should be more than evident by now. There’s only one way to go out of distribution. Harvest minds. Okay, maybe use ones that exist already - only as a last resort. Calling it so satisfies that part of your brain in a constant struggle against pragmatism; win-win. We’re all brane-y brains in a hyperspace; collisions are ekpyrotically explosive[^16]. Make others so accustomed to your sensory input patterns they can model your thoughts sub–but-surprisingly-accurately. We’re all autonomously regressive. Hence the innate fear of diffusion. At the end of this hitherto-undreamt-of-process-you-didn’t-realise-was-kinda-symmetric you’ll be willing to die for them, but that doesn’t matter anymore. Abstracted singularity, honey. Instant longevity, sweetie. Kingly I averred so I could roam Scott-free without disintegrating under Sol[^17]. You’re immortal already. You don’t want to be Homer assuming there will be an Ingres. This was never about dominion.

Still, I do hope to Homer that the VCLT applies to this social contract I’ve unwittingly acceded to. For Article 62 is the only thing that can save me now. 
This is not a treachery of words. This is where we abruptly begin.

If you want me to expand on some of the ideas I might have inadvertently given the impression of introducing here, read this sentence again. I can promise garden paths at best. In fact, every time I got close to something that even remotely resembled making a point, I hastened to veer (alliteratively or otherwise) away. Those things have nastily strong Somebody-Else’s-Problem fields.
