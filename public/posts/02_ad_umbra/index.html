Delenda Sum

Ad Umbra; or, Vampire Strikes Back

August 9, 2025

He must be theory-mad beyond redemption who, in spite of [their]: differences, shall still persist in attempting to reconcile the obstinate oils and waters of Poetry and Truth. (Edgar Allan Poe)

Roses are red Violets are blue If you thought the former green You might be deuteropic too.

I am burning glass bridges. That faint memory of coasting a caustic two,five-dichlorophenol on borosilicate a toast of catharsis. The moat-ley annulus of tranquility around this moltening fortitude an iota of solace. Opening a window. The darkness outside makes my room’s meagre scintillations reach further than I wish. Thinking of Rhapsodic openings, of introductory phrases betraying thematic portents (oh no). Of red herrings. Of this reluctant yearning for cumpescatory naps1 while these weary wares remain vainly void of those vistas of vantablack. Of salmons in no rush to d perish, keeping their monocular vigil against Platonic troglodytes; bending my knees to that nepotic nose-bloodening genuflection at the beginning of a wondrous pilgrimage. Of handing you a Brobdingnagian receptacle of words and pouring in less than a drop of meaning as I rejoice in this most Badr-ic a fashion. Aye, this nigh hill is my abode2, quite humble. Everything’s fair from the vantage of this solipsistic denial of the self.

What am I? Nobody with you. Otis without. Shaft me like he-feasts-us; cannonball-eyes me out3. For once, Wiktionary denies me the leisure of effortlessly peeking behind the veil of this desired adverb. From Latin, duh. C’est tout. Otiose-sus indeed. The paranoid mind sees conspiracies everywhere4. More on, and off, this later. Binary thinking takes me this far and no further. Everyone’s bitten off that slice of pie but belches quite soundlessly.

My sanity, unsurprisingly, fortunately, is in question. The alternative would be far more lamentable, if one stops to not think about it5. Yet the bases – for there are multiple – of the inquisition confound me a little. Is it the departure from the status quo or the ingrained consistency that has only scaled up the cliff face? Can’t even trust One Stone on this one; he clearly never had to make software made by a certain green-eyed quasi-benevolent monster run on hardware made by the monster itself. Cold reboots are underrated. Need one myself; present paradigm not quite supportable. Current and inevitably imminent digressions notwithstanding, the impetus for these long-form obfuscations is not new. Just that ideas that earlier were simply floating around have vermiculated and are now burrowing through my brain like the Shai-Hulud, responding to the incessant beat of pulsating temples. They remain, of course, superficial as ever. The erstwhile tropical mush of grey has been terraformed (spoiler alert?) into an arid system where nothing but this mélange thrives, feeding me its visions in disregardful impunity.

I stare into a pool shaped like a moon shaped like a skull toothlessly, inscrutably, grinning. I fail to dive in before the swarms I’d barely escaped a moment ago engulf me once more and throw me back onto the craggy, slavish shore, that final resting place of all mulled wills; making the imperative of acquiring a capability of Ernest thinking a retrospective absolute. I’m back against the wall, an unwilling fixture in this room – abuzz to the brim with a cackling cacophony of confounded concords to sate constructed compulsions – ruthlessly, irrefutably, spinning. Two whiffs and this miasma floods my insides, somniating everything in its path. Goya’s reason sleeps within me. Without me. Winged monsters, ever the favourites, rise, bound no longer by chains of thought, and dig their talons deep. The spirit squirms but bends as it’s flown out again to the land of fearful asymmetries and bleak silhouettes where eagles don’t dare perch.

Darker perhaps, than expected; or necessary. But then this demesne measured by cards and screens overlooks an environs too bright for me to handle. (Portals revealing the aleatorically negotiable are better left shuttered lest they distract me from the ones that actually matter.) Back to the original question. There has been an inkling of an inclination for inking these incorrigibilities for a while. There may have been better occasions. Maybe I should have paid some heed to that convention-driven motivation to write a lifetime ago. But that vocal flagship uses a scary pronoun. And I never knew whether it signified a collective departure or a departure from the collective. Neither rang true for me in any case6. Resistance is clearly futile though, I have been assimilated at last. So once more I pretend to project semantics by sporadically spewing a simulacrum of syntactically sound sentential units, afraid of what’ll happen if I stop. Terrified of what’ll happen if I continue.

Okay, I guess I do have fears. The greatest perhaps is that my vocabulary might be entirely episodic. That if I were to have a bout of amnesia I would lose what little faculty of communication I have completely. Undeniably irrational, I know it to not be true; my memory is a far cry from decent, eidetic is the whelp of a guinea pig on a planet orbiting Earendel7. But I still can’t help but wonder what if8 at times. It does seem to be true for a large fraction, to be skewedly fair. It’s quite impossible at times to not snap snap snap to the memory most rigorously associated with the word, the phrase, the notion of which I think as my fingers satisfactorily tap tap tap against these tactile keys feeding them back at the behest of a blinking bar behooving uninterrupted motion.

Then there are those mind-crippling moments when a certain phenomenon you have extremely mixed feelings for strikes. The body involuntarily, like clockwork, reacts. That which cannot be holo-stimulated without a replicator registers significant spikes in sensory deviation. You think of the word. You can’t recall it. Years and years of your mom mentioning it in the one situation when you didn’t care about lexicology (lie) have made you impervious, you’re immune to the ability of retention. The word always manages to escape those clutches enfeebled by recalcitrant defiance. A memory of a flavour remains. You’re sure it tastes more made up than most others. A little like Armalcolite. But with more granularity. Flashing a chromatic octave through all that white noise. You know it was named so for a reason. That’s right, it’s a portmanteau. Funny how the French have a different term for this though. (Another cold warning against the dangers of transliteration, but this quintessentially quintilluminiscent blabberwocky is not the place for that discussion.) Yet the roots of this word are older than baguette-langue; baguette-land ipso facto. Latin? Older, vimoodha. Huh? No no, wrong continent. Ah yes. It’s that place. That place with those pigmented loins, with pygmies and lions, with ivory-women-loving – oh quite a bit – sculptors, and all sorts of well-documented (philed?) amateurs. That revered, sanguine land that gave us so many iterations of bloody, the latest perhaps metaphorical. So revered, we don’t have a problem glorifying its creepy bloodlust for rocks every time it rains9.

Enough to give anyone a headache. I’m right there with you. Of late, this cranial torsion has increased in perceived intensity to such a degree it can no longer be ignored. Unquestionably a manifestation of my desire to vanish, to abscond, to have someone wave a wand and have the feeling of an egg cracking on my crown and spilling all the way down. A longitudinal stress compelling me to disapparate, to muster up some: Destination. Determination. Deliberation. There is none. A transverse stress making me wish to simply get beamed up, down, out. Clearly the Frankenstein apprentice assembling me was an Anglophone and misunderstood the instruction to install a cerveau. Hence the high-functioning bobble head. Would explain quite a lot actually.

Like how I can be a little Kurt at times. But my contribution in a conversation I had no clue I was participating in can scarcely be expected to be socially acceptable. I blink in and out, and out about, to find a brimming consciousness, never sure if the earth still feels my gravitational pull or if I’m on a personal Tralfamadore again, certain that the quested after exists on neither. The central when denied gets mistaken for conceit in general. I have this proclivity of rejecting the first candidate response in my head, and moving to the second one; the one without any internal vocabulary, without my inexplicable impressions of the world, without the context for the intended implication, without the suggestion of supposable civility. This dissociation seems nearly physical. Some supreme sorcery statically suspends the spirit from the self, and I want to smack my head, but cogitation still controls the corpo-but-scarcely-real and you end up on the receiving end instead. So it goes10.

And so it stays. All a side-effect of this toxin, math. Has me buzzing like a Phryge; with a cross-begotten pro-anti-ism, like a de-tuned radio; tubes heating up in utter disorder, and no one to fix me by mere thinking, phasing between earworms as if between channels. I do feel summer creeping in, but I don’t think I’ll be tired of this town anytime soon. The recency of the resurgence of the notion that this is nothing but reflective convergence is greater than I’d care to admit. That same old tale of two cities leading to The One. The best of rhymes; the worst of crimes. Literary exile; exanthropy (unsuccessful) masked as scholarly pursuit. (Life; Twins. Hallows; Horcruxes. Dubitable; Deleterious.) A shade following aimlessly in the wake of this nocturnal cradle. Separated, inter alia, through time, we wait at the steps of some museo. No relation to any substring, right Mr. B11? Bit too late for one to be able to find solace in parrotable palatables diminuted in parables when the full glory of Lutetic laetification might be just around the corner. But I worry that I was too late in jumping into this wake, as I feel the vines twined around its evidently sepulchral stumps, and experience yet again the aroma of that earthy cologne of juvenescent neglect. One just wants to have fun. The mental writes all wrongs in a wanton defiance of the fact that reality marches on and one’s pen can’t scribe that which is already obsolete fast enough, one’s mien can’t fabricate wreckless disbandings with masks of reckless abandon12.

Might as well focus on some (more, disjointed) contradictions, no terrestrial reason needed. That which made me can’t unmake me? Cannot, but quite certainly will. Cannot excogitate when trying to correlate contemporary connotations for contended cognates? Much(o) obliged. Can naught place those pearly pillars of creation around me once more? Nothing but time. So I stay, a lone moore-ing on Mars in my finest blues, posin’ for Rodin13 as time passes through me. Funny how its progression can be mistaken so easily for progress. Projections on a semi-infinite axis. The wheel turns in place but the mind keeps tracing phantom cycloids nevertheless. Simpler, perhaps, to just climb up the spokes. One day of extremely low yet just enough probability follows the other and one keeps waiting for today after the morrow14. Waiting for the clocks to strike thirteen.

Where, though, should one want to be when that happens? One would ideally want the shelter of one of the five self-righteously ordained to be. But they were, all of them, deceived, for many others were made; as is the case with objects of Power. A Quirinus Kain stabs the unsuspecting Able minds in the back, yet keeps receiving Nods left and right somehow. Funny how grandfathered stupidity so easily overpowers nascent logic. Funny how people are surprised when deterrence deters nothing but the deterrent. No-first-use doesn’t scale down well, doesn’t scale down at all. Yet you will see this all and still dream of a utopia of semi-definites, the most obvious problem with this construct shrugged off as a paltry detail. That might just be true wisdom though, for clinging to this dream is possibly the only remaining path to REM; its renouncement leads to freakish realisations of how your personal views on the particularities of humanity are precisely the inverse of those of certain subterranean notes-takers, how you’ve been swimming in the murky waters of some twisted transcendentalism, how this one (and perhaps only) disagreement necessitates some gross reassessments of the way things are, how these reassessments require taking a backward step from the said humanity, how you’ve made this impossible by playing a contorted game of the-floor-is-lava, how it most likely would have been easier to demonstrate you’re non compos mentis by taking the sanest course of action - escapism15.

In another such realisation, reminiscent, though in no way worthy, of Pirandello16, one represents the scabbard of the sword they’re trying to extricate from a boulder to prove their worth. Is fulfilling one’s purpose, then, still harakiri? Not unless one is bent on taking things literally because the overheads of inferring the figurative are too high. Funny how these overheads, existent axiomatically for novelties, are acceptable only for a short band between the familiar and the incredible though. One admires oneself for one’s pertinacity but expects you to bring something new to the table every time, as long as it’s nothing too alien. One professes a hatred for musicals yet would more than willingly drown themselves in one of the jukebox variety. Does that indicate a reluctance to give new art a chance? (Nope. Most musicals are unbearable at best. Not like I’m making a bigger point here, or any point, period.) Might have moved from self-immolation to self-absorbed consumption of culture a bit too fast there. But that’s a leap of faith I can no longer deny my peers.

Let me take one more leap and be deliberately cryptic for a minute. Masked Egyptian snake traps Olympians for ascension. Ten letters. A trifecta of terrible morphemes. The suffix, signifying formation; the crime, the root, denoting the former (noun); the criminal, and finally the prefix, an aimless indication of direction; twice adumbrated in this clearly non-temporal guilt. Add a -logy to this one and you see the result is hardly what you want it to be, just what you sometimes may need it to be. (Imprecations provide, I feel, when unaltered, better cor-rections.) I fear mine will be equally empty, when finally made. A small one perhaps, for now. I have hereto used a word indicating the existence of something humorous in a notion on no less than five occasions – in a tone deaf, self-centric mannerism, revealing the extent of dilution of these delighted dabblings, the inexactitude of these unpolished diddlings – in places laughter can hardly be expected. Call me Epi-phunney Khan, the stentorian screamer of words squeezed of all joy, of words left like extinguished torches in sarcophagal puddles, of words left rootless after a premier of The Mark of Zorro, of words evoking exoduses upon epexegeses, of words hidden from the world through the modest pudibundity of the greats who used them.

In the not-yet-evanescent words of another GOAT, something hit me today when that truck didn’t hit me today. The realisation that I didn’t care either way. The absolute lack of a rush of adrenaline. The absence of any bile rising up. The titan I see is dragging me down with it but I don’t fight back; it’s all fine as long as I keep playing this witless fugue in syncopal submission. Nearer, My Gandalf, To Thee. (This fall quite certainly won’t render me white, but one can hope.) All fine as long as Selway, Fab and Sarin keep rhythmically resuscitating the stannic machinery responsible for pumping this emulsion of cold brews and pesto rosso through my veins17. All fine as long as I’m coming second in a race of two roosters.

Because (somewhere bells are swinging nineteen to the dozen in alarm from the way I’ve started this sentence) primus prime is woefully underrated. Take it from someone no longer in their ninth. This societally neutered deuteragony hurts just the same, if not more. Calling it primus inter non-pares would be plagiarism, even if conflicts of interests of time might save me the lawsuit. Let me just call it the prima facie wildly inaccurate premier parmi impairs. End of interlude. Who wants to be the first draft? I want to be New-And-Improved. Iterated and perfected. A giant leap from that rudimentary shell built for cavernous escapes. That’s right, I’ll be Mark II any day. Icarus precarity-contingencies notwithstanding; I am only too evidently playing a riskier Jenga with my hubris already. Mark Two Point, oh then, is my aspirational stylisation. Might want to go a little archaic with the versioning though, slightly imply I’m being split in two veins, one slightly sincere, both equally ridiculous. Might make these not-quite-literary offenses more realistic.

Not that this is a goal in any sense of the word18. I wouldn’t know where to begin. I don’t know what qualifies. Reality is one point six giga-Rashomons and counting. The spotlight is all that counts. So go spell your truth, oh gnostic, lest you may forever be labelled a nastik. The truth (knowledge, wisdom, fruit of education?) has a tendency to wink out faster than you can say apoorvaha ko’pi kosho’yam (okay maybe not FTL then). Better perhaps, to not partake of this supposed panacea. Best not to meddle with this fragile fabric shielding one from the horrors of semantic determinism. Best not to deviate from toeing this line between aphorisms and obscurity – while clearly leaning towards the latter but not for the sake of the latter – in a solitary self-indulgence, in case some part-divine Mr.Real might make one see the need to learn to leggo of the frail Lego leg on which l’ego rests like a lil’ eggo. (I did try. Subtlety is pretty hard. Insert whatever Wilde said about imitation, mediocrity.)

This possibly restricts one from ever reaching the light, but that’s alright - the sun’s just going to burn me anyway. And the way to those shoulders is too steep as it is, too narrow, too demanding. There’s clearly no prospect of comfort. I’d rather just stay here, in the shadows of giants, biding my time and sharpening my claws, chugging Chianti till this hollow fiasco is all that’s left.

(ALL) APOLOGIES

What else should I be?

All caveats from the last one apply, more or less.

Some things are not meant to be read as much as just looked at. Through squinted eyes perhaps. Laughed at hysterically. Spread to others much in the same way a dry twig inadvertently shares a spark just to ensure it’s not the only one having a weirdly, confusingly, inflammatory day.

Some people write in a way that makes you want to transcend time and space and just give them a hug. Others blow your mind so much you just start worshiping the leaves that bear their thoughts. This text was written with the explicit, undiluted knowledge of the fact that neither case possibly applies. In the immortal (and only slightly paraphrased) words of anyone who’s tried to have a conversation with me, it scarcely seems to be supposed to make any sense.

It was in fact – albeit extremely implicitly maybe – an attempt to answer some burning questions troubling no one in particular, such as : Why can’t one just make of oneself an arse, gratia artis, an autotelic pariah amidst tutelary harbingers? Why can’t one just accept the fact that clarity and honesty are far from orthogonal? That perception and reality are equally far from miscible? That one’s vanity invariably makes one choose one over the other? Why can’t one just stop confounding norms with axioms, axioms with theorems, theorems with Theora? And so on.

Also, ignore that autoregressive devolutionism rooting from my worst pun (possibly, so far) in the second paragraph. Ignore that paragraph entirely, and the eleventh – for a minute there, I lost myself. The whole thing, in fact. The footnotes are all I care about anyway (half-truth). Ignore everything in parentheses.

This is a work of fiction. Pronouns remain convenient placeholders for entities with no real being. Real beings are best left undisturbed at the bottom of a well for the time being. The Time Being exultantly lets a lupine howl loose upon finally snatching a Space Being of its own. C’est la vie42.
