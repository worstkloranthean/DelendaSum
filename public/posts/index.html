Glass and peace alike betray proof of fragility under repeated blows. (David Mitchell) Add this blog to that list; a glassy plaque of peevish suppositions of sheepish propositions of peace. Ad Umbra; or, Vampire Strikes Back

He must be theory-mad beyond redemption who, in spite of [their]: differences, shall still persist in attempting to reconcile the obstinate oils and waters of Poetry and Truth. (Edgar Allan Poe) Roses are red Violets are blue If you thought the former green You might be deuteropic too. I am burning glass bridges. That faint memory of coasting a caustic two,five-dichlorophenol on borosilicate a toast of catharsis. The moat-ley annulus of tranquility around this moltening fortitude an iota of solace. Opening a window. The darkness outside makes my room’s meagre scintillations reach further than I wish. Thinking of Rhapsodic openings, of introductory phrases betraying thematic portents (oh no). Of red herrings. Of this reluctant yearning for cumpescatory naps1 while these weary wares remain vainly void of those vistas of vantablack. Of salmons in no rush to d perish, keeping their monocular vigil against Platonic troglodytes; bending my knees to that nepotic nose-bloodening genuflection at the beginning of a wondrous pilgrimage. Of handing you a Brobdingnagian receptacle of words and pouring in less than a drop of meaning as I rejoice in this most Badr-ic a fashion. Aye, this nigh hill is my abode2, quite humble.
